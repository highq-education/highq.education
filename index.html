HighQ

HighQ is the State Academic Wing of Wisdom Students, started with an objective of empowering the society through education. HighQ aims to be part of the acacdemic and career journey of an individual by offering guidance from school level to scholar level.

Education for under-privileged children

Focusing on empowerment of socially, economically, physically and mentally under-privileged children through wholesome education. Click here to register.

Career Clinic - Mentorship

Free one-to-one mentorship for school graduating students to help them choose the right career and get guidance throughout their career. Click here to register.

Scholarship

Financial assitance for students to cover the cost of their education and offer support for nurturing their extra-curricular abilities.

Exam Guidance

Guidance for various Govt. recruitment examinations like UPSC, PSC, SSC.
